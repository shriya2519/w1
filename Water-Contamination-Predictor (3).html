Parameter Details

Total Dissolved Solids

Measures the total amount of mobile charged ions, including minerals, salts, or metals dissolved in water.

Safe range: 0-500 mg/L

Electrical Conductivity

Measures water's ability to conduct an electrical current, directly related to the concentration of dissolved salts.

Safe range: 0-800 Î¼S/cm

pH Level

Measures how acidic or basic water is. The pH scale ranges from 0 to 14, with 7 being neutral.

Safe range: 6.5-8.5

Salinity (Chloride, Sodium, Sulphate)

Measures the concentration of dissolved salt content in water. High levels can affect taste and usability.

Safe range: Cl: <500 mg/L, Na: <200 mg/L

Heavy Metals

Detects presence of toxic metals like lead, mercury, and arsenic that can cause serious health problems.

Safe level: Varies by metal, generally <0.01 mg/L

Bacterial Contamination

Identifies presence of harmful bacteria like E. coli and coliform bacteria that indicate fecal contamination.

Safe level: 0 CFU/100mL for drinking water

Dissolved Oxygen

Measures the amount of oxygen dissolved in water. Essential for aquatic life and indicates water quality.

Healthy range: 5-9 mg/L

Biochemical Oxygen Demand

Measures the amount of oxygen consumed by microorganisms in decomposing organic matter.

Good quality: <3 mg/L

Pesticides and Industrial Chemicals

Detects synthetic chemicals from agricultural runoff or industrial discharge that can be harmful.

Turbidity
